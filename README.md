## üìã Getting Started

### Installation
1. Open Obsidian and go to **Settings ‚Üí Community Plugins ‚Üí Browse**
2. Search for **SystemSculpt AI**
3. Click **Install**, then **Enable**
4. Configure your preferred AI providers in the settings

### Quick Start Guide
1. **Open the AI Chat**: Click the SystemSculpt icon in the left sidebar
2. **Add Context**: Drag and drop files into your chat for reference
3. **Start Chatting**: Ask questions about your notes or generate new content
4. **Explore Similar Notes**: Use the Similar Notes view to discover connections
5. **Customize**: Configure your preferred AI providers and system prompts
